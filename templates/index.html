How it Works

Welcome to the Vision App. This tool allows you to interactively identify and select objects within an image. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload an Image: Click "Choose File" to upload your own picture, or click "Use Sample Image" to test the tool with a default picture.
  2. Automatic Detection: Once loaded, the system will process the image and draw green bounding boxes around all detected objects.
  3. Select an Object: Click anywhere on the image. The app will capture your exact coordinates.
  4. View the Results: The object you clicked will be highlighted with a red box, and detailed processing steps will appear in the table below the image.
